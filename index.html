Caracas - Venezuela

Caracas, the vibrant capital of Venezuela, is a place of natural and cultural beauty, surrounded by the majestic El Ávila National Park. Known for its hospitality, street art, and delicious cuisine, it is also the birthplace of Simón Bolívar, "El Libertador," who liberated six countries in South America: Venezuela, Colombia, Ecuador, Peru, Panama, and Bolivia. Located in the northern part of South America, Caracas holds a special place in history and culture. Additionally, colorful macaws (guacamayas) often grace the city's skies, adding a touch of tropical charm to this already captivating metropolis.
