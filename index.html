About Me

My name is Analina, I was born in Caracas, Venezuela, now I live in Washington, the evergreen state.

I enjoy biking and climbing mountains with my husband, whom I sealed two years ago in a Holy Temple.

I work in an elementary school, I was listening to the children sing "We are Thankful for the USA" and I also feel grateful to live in this beautiful country.

Caracas - Venezuela

Caracas is the capital of Venezuela, it is a valley surrounded by a mountain that separates the city from the Caribbean Sea.

Here was born Simon Bolivar, “The Liberator”, who liberated six countries in South America: Venezuela, Colombia, Ecuador, Peru, Panama and Bolivia.

One of my favorite things are the colorful macaws that live in the mountains and usually decorate the skies of the city, they like to eat bananas and mangos.

For more than 20 years Venezuela has been subjected to a dictatorial government, I pray that we can be free and return and see the beautiful macaws flying around Avila under the blue sky.
